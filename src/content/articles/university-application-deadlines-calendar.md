University application deadlines are not synchronised across countries. Missing a deadline by even one day can mean waiting six months for the next intake. This calendar consolidates the major deadlines.

## UK (UCAS)

- **15 October 2026**: Oxford, Cambridge, most Medicine, Dentistry, Vet Science
- **31 January 2027**: All other undergraduate courses (equal consideration deadline)
- **30 June 2027**: Late applications (clearing)


## US (Common App)

- **1 November 2026**: Early Decision / Early Action (most schools)
- **15 November 2026**: Early Decision / Early Action (some schools)
- **1 January 2027**: Regular Decision (most schools)
- **15 January 2027**: Regular Decision (some schools)


## Canada

- **12 January 2027**: OUAC (Ontario universities) equal consideration
- **Varies**: Other provinces: typically January–March 2027


## Australia

- **December 2026**: UAC (NSW/ACT) on-time applications for Semester 1, 2027
- **May 2027**: Semester 2 intake applications
- **Rolling**: Most universities accept applications year-round for international students


## Netherlands (Studielink)

- **15 January 2027**: Numerus fixus (restricted-entry) programmes
- **1 May 2027**: Non-numerus fixus programmes


## Key Advice

- Apply **at least 2 weeks before** the official deadline to avoid last-minute technical issues
- International students should apply earlier to allow time for visa processing
- For rolling admissions (Australia, many European countries): apply as early as possible within the cycle for the best scholarship opportunities
